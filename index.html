Introduction

Welcome to Defaulter Kon Hai?. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using Defaulter Kon Hai?, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information:

    • Shop name and details
    • Business location information
  • Defaulter Information:
    • Name and contact details of defaulters

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our services

Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase, a secure cloud platform by Google, to store your data. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information.

These services have their own privacy policies and handle your data according to their terms.

Data Sharing

We share defaulter information with other users of the app to help businesses make informed decisions. Personal information of business owners is not shared publicly.
